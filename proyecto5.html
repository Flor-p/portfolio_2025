Identity for a visual artist: ACHE

Concept: The primary element of the brand is the signature of the project’s creator, Ache. Since he is an artist, the goal was to incorporate fluid, non-rigid strokes that also evoke graffiti-style spray paint signatures. A photographic survey was conducted on the streets, and the resulting photos inspired the strokes used for the signature, ultimately forming the brand.
